# 📊 Customer Sales Analytics Dashboard

An interactive **Power BI** dashboard designed to analyze **sales performance, customer behavior, and business trends**. This project transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights using dynamic KPIs, DAX calculations, interactive visualizations, and Year-over-Year (YoY) comparisons.

The project consists of **two interactive dashboards**:

- 📈 **Sales Dashboard**
- 👥 **Customer Dashboard**

# 🚀 Project Overview

This dashboard enables users to:

- Monitor overall sales performance
- Compare Current Year vs Previous Year metrics
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior
- Identify profitable and loss-making product categories
- Track monthly sales and profit trends
- Analyze customer distribution and ordering patterns
- Evaluate business growth using interactive KPIs
- Make data-driven business decisions

# 📈 Sales Dashboard

The Sales Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of sales performance using dynamic KPIs and visualizations.

## Key KPIs

- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Total Quantity Sold
- Current Year vs Previous Year Sales
- Current Year vs Previous Year Profit
- Current Year vs Previous Year Quantity
- Percentage Change in Sales
- Percentage Change in Profit
- Percentage Change in Quantity
- Maximum Monthly Sales
- Minimum Monthly Sales
- Maximum Monthly Profit
- Minimum Monthly Profit
- Maximum Monthly Quantity
- Minimum Monthly Quantity

---

## Sales Analysis

The dashboard includes:

- Monthly Sales Trend
- Monthly Profit Trend
- Average Sales Line
- Average Profit Line
- Sales Growth Analysis
- Profit Growth Analysis
- Quantity Trend Analysis

These visualizations help identify:

- Best-performing months
- Lowest-performing months
- Seasonal trends
- Overall business performance

---

## Product Performance

The dashboard analyzes product performance by **Sub-Category**.

Features include:

- Sales by Sub-Category
- Profit by Sub-Category
- Profit/Loss Indicator

Visualization:

- 🔵 Blue Circle → Profit
- 🟠 Orange Circle → Loss

This helps identify the most profitable and loss-making product categories.

# 👥 Customer Dashboard

The Customer Dashboard focuses on customer behavior and purchasing patterns.

## Customer KPIs

- Total Customers
- Total Orders
- Average Sales per Customer
- Customer Growth (%)
- Order Growth (%)
- Maximum Customers
- Minimum Customers
- Maximum Orders
- Minimum Orders
- Maximum Sales per Customer
- Minimum Sales per Customer

Each KPI compares:

- Current Year
- Previous Year
- Percentage Difference

---

## Customer Analysis

The dashboard includes:

- Customer Distribution by Number of Orders (Histogram)
- Top Customers by Profit
- Customer Purchasing Trends
- Customer Contribution Analysis

These insights help businesses identify:

- Loyal customers
- High-value customers
- Customer ordering behavior

2. Refresh the dataset if required.
3. Explore the dashboards using the interactive filters and slicers.
